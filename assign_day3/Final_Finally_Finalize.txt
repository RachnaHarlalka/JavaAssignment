Difference Between final, finally and finalize :

Final :
 1) Final is a "Keyword" and "access modifier" in Java.
 2) Final is a keyword applicable to classes, variables and methods.
 3) Final method is executed upon its call.

Finally :
 1) Finally is a "block" in Java.
 2) Finally is a block that is always associated with try and catch block.
 3) "Finally" block executes just after the execution of"try-catch" block.

Finalize :
 1) Finalize is a "method" in Java.
 2) Finalize() is a method applicable to objects.
 3) Finalize() method executes just before the destruction of the object.
